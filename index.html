Overview

A quick investment guide through the lens of a highschooler. Monthly updates on where to put your money and where to take it out from. Real-world events have the greatest impact on business cycles and can lead to acute contraction. The goal of investors is to forecast the effects and balance our portfolio in order to maximize profits. Every month, I'm going to drop a quick analysis of the stock market and potential investments of interest with a quick run-down on any real-world issues which are affecting the market.
